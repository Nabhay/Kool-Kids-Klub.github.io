Jobs

As the lead Game Developer for the Kool Kids Klub, your task is to develop scouting and recruitment tools to find worthy soldiers by testing recruits skills such as reflex time, intelligence of aiming skills with some possible genres being: puzzles first person shooters, endless runners etc.

As the lead 3D desginer for the team, your task is to make the exterior of a futuristic building, which will serve as the Headquarters of the resistance, appropriate for the dystopian environment. The base must be inconpicuous, and have hidden fortifications; stratergically place basic models of different weapons, and defense mechanisms on the exterior. You can also add an escape route from the building, in case of emergencies to avoid capture.

As the lead App devloper for the team, you have to create an app for all undercover revolutionists in the resistive force, with
HOME SCREEN displaying the locations of all active operatives and an alert button, which can trigger a change in the UI state or create an alert dialog box.
EMERGENCY SCREEN which consists of the personal data of the user.

Your task as an audio engineer of the team is to create propaganda music that will enlighten the minds of the recruits of Kool Kids Klub. Your music should make the hearts of the listener and create emotions of justice. The song should be about 2-5 minutes, it should be a mp3 file.

As the lead of the Kool Kids Klub, your job is to design graphic and visuals that will serve as the brand image of the Klub. This a very important task because the designs that you make will represent the Klu everywhere; hence, they need to be vissually attractive. you must design:
A logo for the Kool Kids Klub
The Kool Kids Klub Headquarters office needs to put a banner at the entrance, so that the people passing by the office notice its presence.

As the lead motion designer of the Kool Kids Klub, you recognise that while the Klub has vast monetary resources, it will be eventually need extra finances as it becomes larger by recruiting new members to achieve its goal.
Your goal is to make a 25 to 35s of a graphic video to convince the investers to fund us

As the leader of hacking in the resistance to destroy the servers and economy of the Illuminati.

As the lead physical team, you are supposed to attack the forces of the Illuminati. It has many physical forces so do we. There will be a team of snipers and front people with rifles.
